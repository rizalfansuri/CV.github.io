What We Do

Sistem Control

During my internship at PT. ASSI, I worked on an electric motor control panel system project. My responsibilities included designing control systems for various panels, such as the aft hydraulic winch, forward hydraulic winch, cardeck blower, hydrofur sprinkler, stern rampdoor, turning gear, and other panels.

Inspection Electric and Electronic Outfitting (EE0)

I have performed ship inspections during my internship, including performing megger test inspections, checking and testing main and emergency lights, navigation lights, and also installing echosounder depth sensors.

Maintenance Motor Electrical

I have worked on the maintenance and installation of electric motors on KM. Satya Kencana 3 during my internship. During this time, I gained experience with various mechanical tools and measuring instruments.

Desain Wiring Diagram

I have experience in designing wiring diagrams for control systems, ship electrical installations, schematics, and PCB. The design software I am proficient in includes AutoCAD, EasyEDA, Proteus, Eagle, and FluidSIM.

Electro Pneumatic and Hidrolic

"I am developing skills in electro-pneumatic systems, programmable logic control (PLC), and hydraulics. I frequently create system control simulations using FluidSIM and CX-ONE applications before designing wiring diagrams and implementing them.

Electronics and Microcontroller Systems

I have worked on the implementation of a SEPIC converter for a battery charger controlled by a Fuzzy Logic Controller to stabilize the output voltage of the converter. Additionally, I developed a prototype project for a smoke detection and automatic water spraying system with notifications sent via Telegram.

Machine Learning

I utilize Arduino Uno, Google Colab, and Visual Studio Code for this research.
